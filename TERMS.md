## Intended use

CX Notifier provides one-way reminders for supported Codex and Claude Code Hook events. It is not a remote-control or approval system. Every approval or rejection must still be completed in the original coding-agent session.

## Your responsibilities

You are responsible for:

- choosing channels and recipients that are appropriate for the project;
- protecting Webhook URLs, tokens, and signing secrets;
- confirming that notification content is permitted by your organization;
- reviewing third-party provider terms and privacy policies;
- testing the plugin before relying on it for time-sensitive work.

## Availability and warranty

Notification delivery depends on the local machine, network, coding-agent Hook behavior, and third-party providers. Delivery is best-effort and can be delayed or fail.
